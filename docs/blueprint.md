# **App Name**: Ampere Scan

## Core Features:

- Real-time Battery Overview: Display current battery readings including milliamperage (mA) for charge/discharge, voltage, temperature, and percentage charge level.
- Battery Capacity & Health Details: Show the battery's current health status (e.g., 'Bien'), technology type (e.g., 'Li-poly'), and its total maximum design capacity.
- Charge/Discharge Rate & Time Estimation: Calculate and present real-time charging or discharging wattage, instantaneous mA rate, and provide an estimated time until full charge or complete discharge.
- Basic Device Information Display: Present essential device details such as the Android operating system version, the device manufacturer, and the specific model number.
- Smart Battery Optimization Tool: A generative AI tool that analyzes historical and real-time battery usage patterns to provide personalized, actionable recommendations aimed at improving overall battery life and performance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme for reduced eye strain and a sleek, modern look. The design prioritizes performance and technology.
- Primary color: A vibrant, electric blue (#52A3F5) to represent data, energy, and modernity. (HSL: 220, 80%, 60%).
- Background color: A very dark, slightly bluish-grey (#16191C) to provide a deep contrast and professionalism. (HSL: 220, 15%, 10%).
- Accent color: A warm, clear orange (#F5AC46) used sparingly for key call-to-actions, warnings, or highlights to provide strong visual contrast. (HSL: 40, 90%, 70%).
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for its clean, modern, and objective appearance, suitable for precise data presentation.
- Use clean, modern line-art icons, perhaps with subtle gradient fills or accent colors, for all data points. Visual gauges should clearly and attractively represent health, voltage, temperature, and level.
- Implement a modular, card-based grid layout, similar to the reference image, but with optimized spacing and clear visual hierarchy. The most critical data points should be prominently featured at the top of the interface.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for data updates, navigation between sections, and interactive elements. Avoid jarring movements to maintain a professional feel.
